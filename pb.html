About Campus Marketplace

Campus Marketplace is a student-to-student trading platform designed exclusively for university students. Our mission is to create a sustainable, affordable, and convenient marketplace for students to buy and sell academic materials, electronics, dorm essentials, and more.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of university students who were frustrated by the high cost of textbooks and the lack of a dedicated platform for student trading, Campus Marketplace was created to solve these problems. We understand the student experience because we are students ourselves.
